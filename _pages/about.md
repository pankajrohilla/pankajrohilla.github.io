I am an Eckert Postdoc Fellow at the [Bhamla Lab](https://bhamla.gatech.edu/) and [Prausnitz Lab](https://drugdelivery.chbe.gatech.edu/index.html) at the [Department of Chemical and Biomolecular Engineering, Georgia Tech](https://www.chbe.gatech.edu/). I received a Ph.D. from [Department of Chemical Engineering at Texas Tech University](https://www.depts.ttu.edu/che/), advised by [Dr. Jeremy Marston](https://www.depts.ttu.edu/che/faculty/jeremy_marston/index.php).

My research focuses on the development of low-cost hardware for the delivery of mRNA/DNA vaccines and understanding the fluid dynamics of organisms interacting with fluids. My Ph.D. dissertation was focused on understanding the [Hydrodynamics of Needle-Free Jet Injectors](https://ttu-ir.tdl.org/items/c6360889-f859-4b69-b6a4-5e7a4fa7bbe1).
